About Me

I am a postdoc fellow in CMU's Generative Intelligence Lab, working with Professor Jun-Yan Zhu. I completed my Ph.D in computer science at Stanford with Professor Maneesh Agrawala. My research interests lie at the intersection of generative AI, computer graphics, and HCI. In particular, I'm interested in designing new content creation tools to bridge gaps in human perception and attention. In 2015, I received a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences from UC Berkeley, where I did research in geometric modeling with Professor Carlo H. Séquin.
